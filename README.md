# Real Estate Trifecta

Three skills for the three highest-stakes moments in your business: meeting a buyer, winning a listing, and getting a deal from accepted offer to keys. Each one builds finished, branded deliverables, not just talking points.

## What's inside

- **Buyer Consultation** — Walk into every buyer meeting prepared. Builds your pre-meeting brief, the consultation agenda, discovery questions, and a branded Home Buyer Guide the buyer keeps. Saves the contact, books the meeting, drafts the follow-up.
- **Listing Presentation** — Walk in with a pricing story you can defend. Builds the presentation deck you present at the table, the seller leave-behind that works while they decide, and your prep sheet, all from your real comps. Never invents a number.
- **Closing Day** — The map from accepted offer to keys, built for the client's country and region (US and Canada) and their property type. Then the keepsake, the celebration, the gift note, and the 30-day and one-year touches that turn one sale into the next referral.

## How to install

In the Claude desktop app, open the **Cowork** tab and click **Customize**. Click the **+** next to **Personal plugins**, choose **Create plugin**, then **Add marketplace**, then **Add from a repository**, and paste in:

```
PorchLyte/real-estate-trifecta
```

Click the black **Sync** button, then click the **+** on the **Real Estate Trifecta** card to activate it. No zip to download or upload, and you get updates from the same panel whenever they're published.

## How to use it

There's no setup command. Each skill runs a short interview the first time you call on it (four or five questions), saves your answers, and never asks again. Just describe the moment you're walking into:

- "I'm meeting a buyer tomorrow at 10. Here's what she said in her inquiry..."
- "I have a listing appointment Thursday. Here are the comps and the MLS sheet."
- "Offer accepted on 42 Maple! Buyers, closing March 15."

Each skill builds its full package automatically: the deliverables, saved to your Drive, with the calendar events and follow-up drafts offered on top. You review everything before anything goes near a client.

## Connectors that help

All three skills work fully in chat with nothing connected. Connectors are upgrades, not requirements:

- **Google Drive** — every deliverable saves to a clearly named folder per client or listing
- **Google Calendar** — consultations, appointments, contract deadlines, and the 30-day and one-year touches
- **Gmail** — follow-ups and client messages drafted into your inbox; they never send
- **Claude in Chrome** — reads the lead, the comps, or the accepted offer straight off your CRM or MLS screen; read-only, never acts

Connect them under **Customize > Connectors** in Cowork. Each skill will prompt you when one would help.
